FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions about our grants.

What types of grants are available? +
We offer startup grants, small business grants, innovation grants, research grants, and community grants. Each program has different funding amounts and eligibility requirements.
Do I have to repay the grant? +
No, our grants are non-repayable. This is free funding for your business. Unlike loans, there is no interest, no repayment schedule, and no hidden fees.
How long does the process take? +
The application form takes about 5-10 minutes. Our team typically provides a decision within 24 hours. If approved, funds are disbursed shortly after.
Who is eligible to apply? +
Our grants are open to business owners, startup founders, freelancers, non-profits, and entrepreneurs across all industries. You must be at least 18 years old.
How will I receive the funds? +
Approved grant funds are disbursed directly to your bank account via wire transfer or direct deposit. The process is fast, secure, and convenient.
Is there any hidden fee? +
No. Our grants are completely free. There are no application fees, processing fees, or hidden charges.
